Why choose IISR Chandra

Advantages built for real farms

Each feature is chosen to support better establishment, stronger production, and lower avoidable risk.

High Yield Potential

IISR Chandra is known for producing higher dry berry yield per vine than many conventional varieties.

Vigorous Vines

Fast-climbing, healthy growth helps growers establish the plantation sooner with lower early-stage stress.

Uniform Flowering

More synchronized flowering makes harvest cycles easier to plan and manage across the plantation.

Disease Resistant

Its tolerance to quick wilt pressure helps reduce one of the biggest risk points in pepper cultivation.

IISR Certified

Each sapling is linked to authenticated research material, giving growers better confidence in the source.

Expert Support

From batch sizing to planting guidance, growers can reach out over phone or WhatsApp when they need help.

High Piperine Content

Better oleoresin and piperine content can help command stronger value in domestic and export markets.

Fast Establishment

Hardened saplings settle faster after planting and help cut avoidable delays during early growth.
